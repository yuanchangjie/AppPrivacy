01 Collection and Use of Information

Core App Features

To provide our core functionalities, monitor stability, and optimize user experience, the App processes information required to manage, execute, or back up files locally on your device. We request specific device permissions or process localized parameters only when strictly necessary:

Data Type / Permission Purpose & Handling Mechanism
Package / Installed App List Access Used strictly to query and list the applications currently installed on your device, allowing you to interact with or manage them. This information is processed entirely locally on your device and is never uploaded to our servers.
Storage Access (Read/Write) Required to read or write files into your designated device storage directory (e.g., the "Download" folder) based on your explicit operations.
AndroidID Collected as a persistent device identifier. We use it solely to aggregate anonymized, device-level frequency statistics (such as successful operation counts and feature usage trends) to diagnose application stability and maintain quality.
System Environment Details We may collect basic environment telemetry, including your Operating System version, device model, and network connectivity status, to ensure technical compatibility and general functionality.

Crash Analytics and Error Reporting

To identify product bugs and improve application performance, the App may automatically compile runtime logs, stack traces, error codes, and short diagnostic snippets when an unexpected crash or anomaly occurs.
